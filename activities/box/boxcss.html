What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take the width as much as they can and it is showed vertically mean while inline tags take the width for their content only and they do not break the flow as a document.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin means the space of the element that takes on a page. Border means the outer line that surrounds the element's content. Padding means the space of between the content and it's border.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Some units of measurement we can use in CSS are pixels, centimeters, points and percentages.
