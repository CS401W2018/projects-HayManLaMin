Foods of Lower Part of Myanmar

In the lower part of Myanmar, delta region of Ayeyarwaddy River and Thanindary state locates in the Bay of Bangal properous seafoods and rice plantation areas. Cuisines made with freshwater fish and seafoods are main meals. Aside from that the biggest commercial city with the cowdest population, Yangon locates in the lower part of Myanmar. Yangon is a place where all cultures and foods meet together.

Mohinga

Mohinga is national breskfast of Myanmar which is filled with good smell and taste from fish and lemon grass and rice noodle. People usually eat it with Burmese vegetables deep fried and boiled eggs.

Myeik Kat Kyi Kait

Myeik Kat Kyi Kait is the spicy seafoods rice fried noodle which is invented in the costal city called Myeik which locates in Thanindary state.

reference

Shwe Yin Aye

Shwe Yin Aye is one of the national dessert of Myanmar. This sweet and cold dessert is chosen by everyone in the summer.

reference

Spicy dried fish curry with tomato

The delta region of Ayeyarwaddy River is the main region that produces dried fish, dried shrimp and fermented fish. Dried fish cooked with tomato and chili powder give the deep savory taste.
