Foods of Upper Part of Myanmar

In the Upper part of Myanmar, mountains area, many different green herbs are sources for foods. Shan ethic who are descendants from tai people make traditional tai foods which are also famous in Thailand. Chinese style of tofu and mantoo are main breakfast in upper Myanmar. Hunters in the wild forest of Kachin ethic find wild boar as basic meat. Kachin Koaw yay, traditional rice wine which makes Upper Myanmar people to stay warm in the snow.

Shan Noodle

Shan noodle is loved by many tourists because of its savory tomato meat paste, garlic oil and peanuts.

Khawphot or Pounted Rice

The taste of toasting Khawphot with sugar will definitely make someone forget campfire marshmallow.

Kachin Safhaw and Steamed fish

Kachin Safhaq and Steamed fish which are made with many different herbs provide health, and the scent and taste of heaven.

Kachinricetown

In the cold winter, dried meat are main food resources for the people of Upper Myanmar. The taste of Kachinricetown make someone realize that only rice and dried meat can give the best taste.
