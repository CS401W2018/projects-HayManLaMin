Hello, everyone! My name is Hay Man La Min. I am from Myanmar.If you do not know where Myanmar is, no worries. Myanmar is one of the Southeast Asia countries and it is bordered with Thailand and China. I finished my high school in the City of Yangon in Myanmar. And then I served as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for one year and six months in the land of smile, Thailand. After finished serving my mission, I went back to my home country and worked as a Korean language teacher for a few months.Now I am studying in the Brigham University-Hawaii.

When talking about myself, it is impossible not to talk about K dramas and K variety shows. I enjoy watching them alot. As I am the only child, I had to spend most of my times alone. Television was my best friend. Everynight after our shop was closed, my grandmother and me watched K dramas together. Those memories were unforgettable.Those memories helped me to learn Korean language and build my interest in making YouTube videos and I made my first ever video last Saturday.
