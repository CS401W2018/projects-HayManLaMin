Prince Eric

Prince Eric is one of the characters in the Little Meramid story, written by Hans Andersen. He is the prince of the seaside kingdom and fulled with passion of sailing. He got married with the mermaid Ariel who rescued him from drowning and became the father of Melody.

Melody
