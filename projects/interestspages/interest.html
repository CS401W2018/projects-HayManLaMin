'The Fiery Priest' is a Korean drama series which was produced and on aired by the Korean television channel 'SBS.' The first season of the series was aired in 2019 and the second came out again in 2024. Some famous korean celebrities who are Kim Nam-gil, Kim Sung-kyun, and Lee Hanee with Ko Jun and Keum Sae-rok did their best to get one of the best dramas. The first season won some prizes and it got the highest viewers rate in the year of 2019. The popularity of the first season made the second happened.

'The Fiery Priest' is about a Catholic Priest who can not control his anger but has a good heart and try to fight with all bad people and save poor people who do not get protection by the Law or authorized people who have responsibilities. Along the way of his hero mission, he got to meet a police and an inspector who have the same purpose with him. This drama is designed with amazing action sences but in the same time, viewers will get to laugh so bad because of talented acting skills of all starrings.
