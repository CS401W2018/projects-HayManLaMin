Kim Nam Gil

Interest Hero

Kim Nam Gil was a main actor and played the role of the angry Catholic priest who used to be a NIS agent and is really good at fighting. A 45 years old actor played not only in The Fiery Priest but also in many Movies, Series and Musicals and won some prizes in his life.

Prizes Kim Nam Gil won
BaekSang Arts Best New Actor 2010, Grand Prize of SBS Drama Awards 2019, 2022 and 2024.
Education of him
He went to Han Yeong High School and studied 'Theatre and Movies' at Myeong Ji College. He could not finish his study but had to quit because of his busy schedule.
Hobbies of this famous star
He enjoys various hobbies like the flute, tap dancing, water skiing, and Taekwondo.
