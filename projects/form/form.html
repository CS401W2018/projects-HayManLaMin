Web Design

1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

When we submit the form, it specifies the URL that the form goes.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

The method decides the way which the form data is sent to the server.

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

We use "Post" when we do wanna post something and when we do not want our input data in the form will be showed up in URL. We use "get" when we want to read and search data.
